Computer Engineering

In our school, technical course is one of the facility that is advantage for the students. They learn different subjects like Web designing, Electronics devices and circuit, Computer network and along with these they also learn different programming languages as well. Each subject have their own scope for the bright future of the students. This course aims to produce professionally able manpower in field of computers.

Actually, the duration of this course is five years with four years theoretical classes and one year practical but in order to reduce the duration the course is arrange in such a way that it is completed with the completion of grade 12. This allows the students to study further without wasting their precious time.

What Makes Our Program Special

What Does The Course Include?

  • Basic concepts and theories about analysis, system implementation, operation, and maintenance of various application and systems of Computer.
  • Designing, Implementing, Programming, Verifying and Analyzing Computing System.
  • Application of Software, Operating System, Hardware and Circuit.
  • Creating a wide array of computing system and devices.

Scopes of the Course

The course sets the foundation in order to make students capable of pursuing different sectors of computer industry as software engineers, programmers, computer system architects, microprocessor/ hardware, designers, among many other specialties.
