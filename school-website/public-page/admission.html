Admission Requirements

Documents Required

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original & copy)
  • Previous school transcript/marksheet
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Parent/Guardian ID copies
  • Medical certificate

Academic Criteria

  • Nursery-Grade 5: Age appropriate admission
  • Grade 6-8: Pass certificate from previous grade
  • Grade 9: SEE certificate or equivalent
  • Grade 11: SLC/SEE certificate with required grades
  • Entrance test may be conducted
  • Interview with parents/guardians

Age Requirements

  • Nursery: 3-4 years
  • LKG: 4-5 years
  • UKG: 5-6 years
  • Grade 1: 6-7 years
  • Other grades: Age appropriate
  • Maximum age limit as per government norms
