    SCIENCE LAB

    Science is the study of the nature and behaviour of natural things and the knowledge that we obtain about them. Laboratory experiences are essential for students in many science cources. Science Labs provide students with various opportunities to learn and experinment new and exisiting things.

    MAKER SPACE

    A makerspace is a room that contains tools and components, allowing an idea and discover insights to implement the idea. She/He practises critical thinking skills, challenges their imaginations, and come up with solution for the real-world problems.

    FOOD FACILITY

    Our school provides lunch for the students from playgroup to grade 8. Food is provided to the students according to the schedule. We provide healthy foods like: Fruits, Vegetables, milk, biscuits, etc. They are enriched in nutrients for the kids. All the schools should be responsible for students'health.

    COMPUTE LAB

    Our school is well equipped with many facilities. One od them is a computer lab with advanced computers. We do many programs in language like C, C++, HTML, CSS, Java, etc. Students from 1st to 12th have opportunities to do programming. It is the space where an entire class can be taught improtant concepts of computer.

    ELECTRONIC LAB

    Electronic lab is another significant attraction where we construct electronic circutis using analog or digital ICs. The experinments covered in this laboratory are synchronized with its theoretical part so that students might be able to understand its practical part. The laboratory emphasizes to gain hands-on experiences of electronic components.

    MEDICAL

    When we have minor injuries,illness,mensuration or other health concerns during school hours, the medical room provides a convenient and cozy accessible location where students can rest. We get different medicines according to the illness we face at school.

    HEALTH CHECK-UP

    Regular health checkups at our school is a common tradition that has benefited many students. Doctors,health specialists and health personal make frequent visits and suggestions to students,and staffs. They follow the given recommendations. As health is one of the important factor in ones life, our school has shown sincere about it.

    EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITY

    Extracurricular activities are all the activities that are organised outside the regular school activity for meeting diverse students'interests and abilities. The school holds different activities that make students active,joyful and energetic. These activities encourage students to expose themselves to the outer world.

    LIBRARY

    Our school provides wide range of books including science, literature, famous personalities, economics, commerce, etc. It is a quite and peaceful place to study. Our studentsshow interests and love to visit the library, read and enjoy books.
